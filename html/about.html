Our History

Founded in 1998, DiamondCut Motors has grown exponentially over the years, becoming a premier luxury car reseller in Indonesia. Our journey began with a vision to bring the world's most prestigious automobile brands to discerning customers in Indonesia. Today, we proudly offer an exclusive range of renowned brands such as Porsche, Mercedes-Benz, Bugatti, and many others, catering to the sophisticated tastes of our esteemed clientele.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the premier luxury car reseller, both in Indonesia and globally, recognized for our exclusive collection of prestigious automobile brands and our exceptional customer experience.

Unmatched Inventory and Service

We aim to offer an exclusive collection of the world's most prestigious automobile brands paired with exceptional customer service that exceeds expectations.

Global Expansion and Recignition

We strive to expand our reach to successful individuals and business owners worldwide, becoming the go-to destination for luxury car enthusiasts and enhancing our global recognition.

Innovation and Market Leadership

Through continuous growth and innovation, we aim to drive significant revenue growth and set new benchmarks in the luxury car resale market, solidifying our position as industry leaders.

Our Mission

DiamondCut Motors aims to offer top-tier luxury vehicles paired with exceptional customer care, providing an unmatched buying experience. Our commitment lies in delivering excellence, safety, and lasting value to our clientele.

Cutting-Edge Service

Offer an unparalleled level of service that is innovative, forward-thinking, and tailored to meet the unique needs of our customers, ensuring a seamless and memorable experience.

Timely Delivery

Prioritize efficient and prompt delivery processes, ensuring that our customers receive their vehicles in a timely manner without compromising on quality or safety.

Steadfast Focus on Vehicle Safety

Uphold the highest standards of safety in every aspect of our operations, from vehicle selection and maintenance to customer education and support, ensuring peace of mind and trust for our clientele.
